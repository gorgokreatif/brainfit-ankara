PROJECT GOAL
Create a website for BrainFit Ankara, a parent-facing, trust-building, premium but warm cognitive development center website. The target audience is mostly parents of children aged 4–18 who are worried about attention, focus, reading, writing, academic performance, exam stress, learning difficulties, DEHB/ADHD, dyslexia, memory, confidence and emotional resilience.

The site must not feel like a generic school website, generic clinic website, childish kindergarten website or SaaS template. It must feel like a premium “cognitive development studio” where science, movement, child development and parent trust meet.

DESIGN CONCEPT
Create an unusual but elegant design system called:
“Zihin Haritası Deneyimi / Cognitive Journey Interface”

Use the BrainFit logo colors as a modular navigation and section identity system. Each cognitive area gets its own visual identity:
1. Dikkat & Odaklanma — blue
2. Görsel Beceriler — yellow
3. İşitsel Beceriler — magenta
4. Psiko-Motor Beceriler — orange
5. Sosyal-Duygusal Beceriler — green

The design should feel:
- warm
- premium
- scientific
- parent-friendly
- Ankara-local but not provincial
- clean but not empty
- modern but not futuristic
- emotional but not manipulative
- trustworthy but not cold

AVOID
Do not use generic blue gradients.
Do not use fake neon brain holograms.
Do not use overused stock “child holding book” layouts.
Do not use hospital-style cold visuals.
Do not use childish cartoon mascots.
Do not use generic Elementor-like section blocks.
Do not use dark sci-fi UI.
Do not make medical treatment claims.
Do not write “tedavi eder”, “iyileştirir”, “kesin çözüm”, “garanti sonuç” language.
Use “destekler”, “gelişimi hedefler”, “bireysel ihtiyaçlara göre yapılandırılır”, “kök nedeni anlamaya yardımcı olur” language.
